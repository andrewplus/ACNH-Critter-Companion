Data Sources

The Animal Crossing Critter Companion web app uses a variety of sources for its information and images. Critter data is provided open-source on Github under CC-BY-SA for both personal use and in other projects.

Critter names and stats: Adapted from the Animal Crossing Wiki on Fandom, converted to JSON, and cross-checked with various other online guides and the game itself.

Critter renders and icons: Courtesy of ElChicoEevee and Alexis's Google Drive folder.

Additional research: The collaborative Animal Crossing: New Horizons spreadsheet

Accuracy Statement

Though every effort has been made to verify the data used by this website, its accuracy cannot be guaranteed. If you spot an error, please fill out the feedback form.
